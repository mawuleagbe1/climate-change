Take Action for Ghana's Climate

How you can contribute to climate solutions in Ghana

What You Can Do As An Individual

Every Ghanaian can make a difference through daily choices and actions:

Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

  • Use energy-efficient appliances and LED bulbs
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle waste
  • Choose walking, cycling, or public transport when possible
  • Conserve water in your home

Make Sustainable Choices

  • Buy local, seasonal produce to reduce food miles
  • Reduce meat consumption, especially beef
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Choose sustainable seafood options

Green Your Space

  • Plant native trees in your community
  • Start a home garden for food security
  • Create compost from organic waste
  • Install rainwater harvesting systems

Community Action

Communities across Ghana are taking collective action to address climate change:

  • Form environmental committees to address local climate challenges
  • Establish community forests to protect and restore ecosystems
  • Develop early warning systems for floods and other hazards
  • Promote climate-smart farming techniques through farmer field schools
  • Create alternative livelihoods that reduce pressure on natural resources
  • Organize education campaigns on climate adaptation

Business and Organization Action

Companies and institutions have a critical role to play in climate action:

Green Your Operations

  • Conduct energy audits and implement efficiency measures
  • Switch to renewable energy sources
  • Reduce water consumption and improve wastewater management
  • Implement sustainable waste management practices
  • Green your facilities with plants and reflective surfaces

Sustainable Supply Chains

  • Source materials and products sustainably
  • Work with suppliers to reduce their environmental impact
  • Prioritize local suppliers to reduce transportation emissions
  • Eliminate deforestation from supply chains
  • Implement circular economy principles

Sustainable Products/Services

  • Develop climate-friendly products and services
  • Provide information to help customers make sustainable choices
  • Design products for durability and recyclability
  • Offer repair and take-back programs
  • Innovate to help customers reduce their carbon footprint

Climate Advocacy

  • Publicly commit to climate action goals
  • Engage in policy discussions on climate issues
  • Educate employees and customers about climate change
  • Join business climate action networks
  • Report publicly on your climate performance
